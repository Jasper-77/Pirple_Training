Bread French Toast by Jas

Yummy Bread slices

Delicious and easy egg breakfast recipe made with bread.Bread French toast is a popular classic breakfast that can also be served as a snack.

I like bread very much.That's why I have prepared this food.

Ingredients

  • Bread slices
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Egg
  • Cinnamon powder
  • Honey

Directions

  1. Take a bowl 🥣
  2. Crack the eggs and beat it 🥚
  3. Add sugar, milk and stir the mixture 🥛
  4. Add a pinch of Cinnamon powder for flavour
  5. Dip the bread slices in the mixture 🍞
  6. Toast the bread slices with butter using bread toaster 🍳
  7. Serve it hot with honey 🍽️
